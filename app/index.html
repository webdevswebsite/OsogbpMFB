LendGap is a simple peer-to-peer loans platform that enables lenders looking to invest funds and people in need of funding to connect and easily manage their loan transactions.

Learn More

  • Make a Request

    Create a loan request from anywhere, at any time, with your own payback terms.

  • Receive Payment

    Interested lenders review and accept your request. You get paid instantly.

  • Repay Your Loan

    Make repayments on or before due date. On-time repayment increases your access to better loan offers.

Why LendGap?

  • Safe Transactions

    Lenders and Borrowers on LendGap are verified and loan transactions are thoroughly scrutinized.

  • Quick Deals

    LendGap offers you easy access to quick loans so you can execute transactions quickly.

  • Made With Love
